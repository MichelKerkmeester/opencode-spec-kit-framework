## Example 1: Product Deal — HEMA Fashion (PASS)

### Template Output

**Title:** Receive a HEMA Fashion Package (€95 value)

**Deal Description:**
HEMA is one of the most recognized lifestyle brands in the Netherlands, known for affordable quality across fashion, home and everyday essentials. For this collaboration, you'll receive a curated fashion package featuring seasonal favorites from their latest collection.

The package is ideal for creators who love styling content, outfit-of-the-day videos or casual fashion hauls. Whether you mix HEMA pieces with your existing wardrobe or do a full unboxing, there's plenty to work with.

**You will receive:** 1x HEMA Fashion Package with a total value of €95.

**Creator Requirements:**

**What we ask in return:**
- 1x Instagram Reel or TikTok (minimum 30 seconds)
- Show the products in use (try-on, styling, outfit creation or unboxing)
- Keep the content authentic and in your own style
- Tag @hema in both the caption and the content
- Provide Spark Ad code (TikTok) or Partnership Ad code (Instagram)

**Optional Add-ons:**
- **Spark Ads / Partnership Ads**
    Allows HEMA to boost the creator's post as an ad for extra reach and performance.
- **Content usage rights**
    HEMA may reuse the content on its website, socials or ads.
    *(We recommend receiving the content through WeTransfer to maintain the quality)*

## Example 4: Variation Demo — Rituals Surprise Box (3 Variations)

This example demonstrates the 9/6/3 variation scaling applied to the description section only. The title, creator requirements, and add-ons remain fixed across all variations.

**Fixed Title:** Receive a Rituals Surprise Box (€150 value)

**Most concise:**

Rituals is a premium home and body care brand known for luxurious products inspired by ancient traditions. For this collaboration, you'll receive a curated Surprise Box with a total value of €150, including bestselling body scrubs, candles and shower foams. Perfect for unboxing content, self-care routines or product review videos.

**Most valuable:**

Rituals creates premium self-care products that blend ancient rituals with modern luxury. The brand has over 900 stores worldwide and a dedicated following among lifestyle creators. Your Surprise Box (€150 value) includes 6-8 full-size products from their most popular collections. Each product is designed to create a sensory experience, making it ideal for detailed review content, ASMR-style unboxings or morning routine videos where you can showcase multiple items in depth.

**Most authentic:**

If you love self-care products that look as good as they feel, Rituals is a strong fit. The brand focuses on turning everyday routines into meaningful moments, and their packaging and product design reflect that philosophy. You'll receive a hand-picked Surprise Box worth €150 with products ranging from their signature body cream to scented candles. This works well for creators who want to weave products naturally into lifestyle content rather than doing a straightforward product review.

### Why These Differ

| Aspect | Most concise | Most valuable | Most authentic |
|--------|----------------------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| Word count | ~55 | ~95 | ~85 |
| Focus | Quick facts, product list | Brand credibility, content depth | Personal fit, natural integration |
| Tone | Direct, informative | Data-backed, comprehensive | Conversational, relatable |
| Best for | Experienced creators who need minimal context | Creators evaluating the opportunity | Creators who prefer organic content styles |
