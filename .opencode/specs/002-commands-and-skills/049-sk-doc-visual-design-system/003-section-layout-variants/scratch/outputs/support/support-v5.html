01
Inspect the wrapping container in DevTools. Ensure min-width: 0 is applied to CSS Grid or Flexbox children.
02

Contextual FAQ

Can I skip diagnostic steps?
No. Owners require the output of all prior diagnostic commands to establish a baseline before they can assist with escalation.
What if my issue category is missing?
For novel or unmapped issues, default to the "Platform Operations" lane and document your reproduction steps in a new incident report.
How is the severity tag determined?
Tags correlate with the maximum potential blast radius of the issue. Deployment issues block merges, making them critical by default.
Are response times guaranteed?
Self-serve resolution is immediate. Escorted responses depend on the on-call rotation schedule and the applied severity tag.
